Services Overview

What AI services does Mayur Prabhune offer?

Mayur offers three core AI mentoring programs: AI for Startups Bootcamp (comprehensive AI implementation for startups), AI Leadership for Managers (strategic AI integration and leadership training), and AI Awareness for Students (foundational AI knowledge for beginners). Each program is tailored to specific audience needs and skill levels.

Who can benefit from Mayur's AI mentoring services?

Mayur's services cater to three main audiences: startup founders and entrepreneurs looking to integrate AI into their business models, managers and executives who need to lead AI initiatives effectively, and students or professionals seeking to understand AI fundamentals and career opportunities in artificial intelligence.

What makes Mayur's AI training approach unique?

Mayur combines practical implementation with strategic thinking, focusing on ethical AI deployment and real-world applications. His programs emphasize hands-on learning, industry best practices, and personalized mentorship to ensure participants can immediately apply AI knowledge in their specific contexts.

How does the AI for Startups Bootcamp help businesses?

The bootcamp provides startups with AI strategy development, practical implementation roadmaps, cost-effective AI solutions, and market positioning guidance. Participants learn to identify AI opportunities, evaluate AI tools, build AI-ready teams, and create competitive advantages through intelligent automation.

What topics are covered in AI Leadership for Managers?

The program covers AI strategy formulation, team leadership in AI projects, change management for AI adoption, ethical AI implementation, ROI measurement for AI initiatives, and building AI-literate organizations. Managers learn to bridge technical and business perspectives effectively.

Is the AI Awareness course suitable for complete beginners?

Yes, the AI Awareness program is designed for complete beginners with no prior AI experience. It covers AI fundamentals, practical applications, career opportunities, ethical considerations, and hands-on exercises using user-friendly AI tools. Students gain confidence and practical skills to continue their AI journey.
